Venue Travel-Time Reference

Pre-calculated one-way drive times from CHS and the resulting billable dead-head (after the 15-min-per-leg waiver, round trip). Use for fast quoting; confirm with live traffic for the actual booking time.

Venue ▾ Area One-way (min) Billable dead-head (hrs) Bus route
Billable dead-head = round trip, minus 15 min waived per leg, expressed in hours. A venue ≤15 min each way contributes ~0 billable dead-head.

Drive times are typical-condition CAR estimates from CHS. For buses, apply the time adjustments: standard bus ×1.15 on drive legs; vintage bus ×1.25 on highway routes (flagged "Hwy") or ×1.15 in-city; +10 min per stop; +15 min if a downtown/historic pickup or dropoff is involved. The Kiawah / Johns Island corridor swings widely with traffic (Ocean Course 55–80+ min) — always confirm with a live traffic check for the booked date and time. Entries marked "verify" are best-available estimates; confirm against Google Maps. Add venues as you book them.
